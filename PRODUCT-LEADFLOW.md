# LeadFlow - SMB Lead Automation

## Problem
- SMBs lose money on missed leads
- Slow follow-up kills deals
- Inconsistent booking = revenue loss
- No resources for fancy CRM

## Solution
Simple lead follow-up automation:
1. Web form captures leads
2. Instant notification to owner
3. Automated follow-up sequences
4. Booking integration

## Features
- Lead capture form (embeddable)
- WhatsApp/Telegram/SMS notifications
- Auto-follow-up sequences
- Calendar booking link

## Pricing
- Free: 50 leads/month
- Pro: $29/mo - unlimited leads, sequences

## Target
Small businesses, freelancers, solopreneurs

MVP READY - Working lead capture & follow-up
