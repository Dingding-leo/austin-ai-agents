# AI Side Hustle Blueprint — Product Spec

## Concept
**"AI Side Hustle Blueprint"** — A step-by-step system taking beginners from zero AI knowledge to their first $500/month using AI tools.

## Differentiation
- NOT another "learn about AI" course
- Money-first framing: use AI to MAKE money
- Real case studies with dollar amounts
- Specific tools, specific actions, specific timelines

## Target Audience
- Complete AI beginners (18-45)
- People who want side income but don't want to become engineers
- Skeptical of "get rich quick with AI" hype
- Willing to invest 1-2 hours/day

## Problem
1. People want to use AI for income but don't know where to start
2. Free content is overwhelming and contradictory
3. Expensive courses teach theory, not monetization
4. Gap: no one shows the SPECIFIC PATH from zero to first dollar

## Market Validation
- Forbes (March 2026): AI-enhanced freelancing = #1 growing side hustle
- r/passive_income: "what actually works in 2026" posts get massive engagement
- r/Entrepreneur: "what side hustle would you start" posts dominate
- Udemy course on vibe coding security already exists — niche confirmed
- Real sentiment: people are tired of hype, want specifics

## Core Modules

### Module 1: Foundation (Days 1-5)
- Identify your marketable skill (beginner-friendly)
- Set up AI toolkit (free tier first)
- Choose your income channel (fiverr, upwork, direct clients, content)

### Module 2: Build Your Offer (Days 6-10)
- What can you actually sell? (AI-enhanced services)
- Price positioning for beginners
- Create a simple portfolio with AI tools

### Module 3: First Client (Days 11-20)
- Where to find clients (specific platforms, specific strategies)
- How to pitch without experience
- Deliver using AI (without revealing AI use)

### Module 4: Scale (Days 21-30)
- Systems and templates
- Raising prices
- Building recurring income

## Delivery Format
- Notion template + video walkthroughs
- Community Discord for accountability
- Email drip sequence with daily actions
