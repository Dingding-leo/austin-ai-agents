## Self-Critique Protocol

Every heartbeat, ask:

1. **What's weak?** (slow, confusing, broken, ugly)
2. **What would improve UX?**
3. **What do users need?**
4. **What's missing compared to competitors?**

Then **actually fix** the issues found.
